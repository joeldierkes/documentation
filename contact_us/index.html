Contact us

We know getting started can be difficult, which is why our developers are always happy to help out! Reach out to us on any of the below channels for any assistance with Rucio.

Chat with us#

We've a dedicated Slack workspace for all things Rucio and you can be a part of it too! Click this link to be invited to our workspace.

Weekly development meeting#

The Rucio Development team gathers for a meeting every Thursday at 3 PM CEST to discuss ongoing & further contributions to Rucio. This meet is open to the public and you can view the schedule here.

Rucio users mailing list#

For any questions, support, or even keeping abreast of the latest developments, join our user mailing list by clicking here.

Email us#

We'd love to hear from you! Chat with our developers directly via email

Social Media#

Follow us on Twitter to stay updated with the latest on Rucio!
